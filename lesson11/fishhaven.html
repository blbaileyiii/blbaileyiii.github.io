    Harbor Attack

    by B L Bailey III

    Last night's storm left citizens of Fish Haven shaken as monsterous forms emerged from the water and ransacked the town. Local authorities were able to push the monsters back to the watery depths from whence they came but not without heavy injury. Sheriff Boggs said, "Bullets just didn't seem to work. They didn't like pepper-spay none to much, but bullets were basically useless. We finally had to set up barracades and roll gasoline barrels at them. That's how we drove them back. Messy business." Authorities are now looking for clues as to who might have summoned these eldritch horrors. Clean up is underway, though the coast line is littered with rusted ships that had sunken years ago, brought to the surface by the storm and our unwelcome visitor. Efforts to remove the debree is likely to take months.
