    Jeff's Diner May Shutdown

    by Lee Bailey

    COVID-19 doesn't seem to care if you are human or not. Today Jeff announced that he is considering shutting his doors for good. Jeff's Diner has long been a pillar in this community offering baked goods, icecream, and Jeff's mother's famous recipes for generations. But Covid has recently stuck Jeff's Diner and hard. Jeff and his wife Debbie are both sick and must now quarantine. "Business was already slow, what with only being able to rely on delivery or take out," Jeff lamented early this Friday, "but now that Debbie and I are sick too... I don't know how we can keep the doors open and the business afloat. It's going to take a miracle. That's for sure." But a miracle might just be in Jeff's future. Mayor Bronsby is issuing stays on debts and liens that will likely allow Jeff to pick up right where he left off once he is able to open his doors again.
