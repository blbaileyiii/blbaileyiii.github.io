Target-Audience

Our target audience is mostly younger kids, and adults who want to simply see the basic weather at a glance. Our goal is to present the data as simply as possible so a five-year old can tell his/her mother that it is going to rain today, and his/her mother can check and say yes it is in fact going to rain.

Personas

Five-year Old Boy:

  • Age: 5
  • Gender: Male
  • Education Level: Starting Kindergarten
  • Internet Experience: Low-Moderate
  • Access to the site: Phone, Table, Laptop
  • Motivations: Food

Scenarios

  • Is it going to be a snow day?
  • Can I play baseball after school?
  • Should I take an umbrella/raincoat with me to school?
  • Will I be able to play soccer at recess?
  • Will my birthday party be ok outside?

Mother of Three:

  • Age: 35
  • Gender: Female
  • Education Level: College Graduate
  • Internet Experience: Extensive
  • Access to the site: Phone, Table, Laptop
  • Motivations: Ease of use, and time.

Scenarios

  • Is it raining downtown tonight?
  • Can we go to the beach this weekend?
  • Will it be too hot to work in the garden?
  • Do I need to water my plants or will it sprinkle today?
  • Do we all need to leave for work a bit early because of the weather?
