Stingray Beach

Cozumel Stingray Encounter & Snorkel

Everybody loves friendly Rays! Enjoy two great ray activities -a personal encounter plus shore-side snorkeling. Then relax and have fun at a private shore club with its resident, friendly critters, and water trampoline. Mere minutes from your ship, you’ll be provided with snorkeling gear and briefed on equipment and the rays. Your guide works with small groups to give you personal attention.

First, you’ll encounter the rays in a shallow area and get to feed them if you like. Then, you'll move to a larger area for snorkeling with a larger school of rays and all their colorful fish friends. Finally, you’ll have some beach club leisure time, complete with a snack bar, souvenirs and photos available to complete your experience.

Down below!

From inside our Cessna airplane, you’ll gain new appreciation for the majesty and beauty of Cozumel Island.

Take a memorable look at the amazing turquoise blue of the Caribbean Sea, Passion Island, Punta Molas, Cozumel Wild Side, Punta Sur, Cozumel Marine Park coral reefs, historic El Cedral, and our famous Cozumel beaches!
