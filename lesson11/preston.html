    Balloon Festival

    by Sulika Davis

    The only thing prettier than a clear rainbow is clear skies filled with an array of hot air balloons. This year Preston's balloon festival takes the cake as more than 2,000 balloons prepare for take off. Participants from around the globe travel to Preston Idaho to launch their balloons from Founder's Park. They set up in the earliest morning hours, many of them arriving before 3am. Then they begin to unroll the kaleidoscope fabric until they are ready to begin turning on the hot air. While it only takes 10 or 15 minutes to fill the balloon, it takes considerably more time to properly untangle lines, fabric, and secure it all to the ground. After that is the you have to get the proper clearance to launch and more. But the results are stunning to watch.
